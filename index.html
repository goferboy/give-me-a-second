Give Me A Second!

Welcome to "Give Me a Second!"

A 2-Player "Guess That Tune" game where you only have one second to figure out the song!

You and your opponent must buzz in after a song plays and correctly guess which song it is.

Guess right, you gain 100 points, but guess wrong, and you lose 100! Who ever has the highest score when the game ends, wins!

How to play:

When both players are ready, click on the play button. After the countdown the song will play

Four possible songs will then pop up. If you know what it is, you must buzz in before your opponent does

Player one's buzzer is the key 'A' and Player two's buzzer is the key 'L'

After buzzing in, select the corresponding you want, (1-4).

Feeling stumped? Every player has one chance to use a 50/50 power up, where two of the wrong answers will be removed!
