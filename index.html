Give Me A Second!

Welcome to "Give Me a Second!"

A 2-Player "Guess That Tune" game where you only have one second to figure out the song!

You and your opponent must buzz in after a song plays and correctly guess which song it is. Guess right, you gain 100 points, but guess wrong, and you lose 100! Who ever has the highest score after five rounds, wins!

How to play:

  • When both players are ready to begin the game, click on 'Begin Game!' in the upper right.

  • The main display will show up, showing Player 1's side on the left, and Player's two side on the right.

  • When both players are ready, hit the center Play icon.

  • After a countdown of three, the song sample will play and the round will begin

  • After the song plays, 4 possible choices will appear, with only one of them correct

  • A timer bar will also start to shrink from green to red.

  • Each player has their own buzzer, Player 1's is the 'A' Key and Player 2's is the 'L' Key .

  • Before the timer bar shrinks and disappears, a play must buzz in and select one of the answers by hitting the '1', '2', '3', or '4' key

  • If they get it right, they get 100 points! But if they get it wrong, they lose 100!

  • After an answer has been selected or the timer runs out, the play button will re-appear. Click on it when you're ready for the next round.

  • This will continue till all 5 rounds have finished. Winner will have the most points

  • Each player also has a pair of single-use power ups they can use!

  • To use them, a player must first buzz in before the timer is gone, then press the respective key corresponding to the power up they want to use. Remember: you can only use each one ONCE!

  • 50/50 ('C' Key ): Need help deciding? This will remove two wrong answers.

  • Nail ('N' Key ): Thinks your opponent doesn't know the answer? Bet on it! Using this option forces your opponent to answer. If they get it wrong, they lose 100 points, and you gain 100! But be careful, if they get it right, they take your 100 points from you!

  • After finishing a game, click 'Next Game!' to play again! See who can get the most wins!
